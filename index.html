How It Works

  • There are two buttons: Button A (blue) and Button B (purple)
  • Both buttons do the same thing: They reset the timer to zero
  • Your choice is tracked: We record which button you press
  • The mystery: Does your choice matter? Is there a difference?
  • Global sync: Everyone sees the same timer and stats
  • Try to resist pressing either button... or don't!

Keyboard Controls

  • A key or Left Arrow: Press Button A
  • B key or Right Arrow: Press Button B
  • Space or Enter: Randomly press A or B
Warning: Pressing EITHER button will reset the timer for everyone!
